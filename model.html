For millennia — even before farming —

humans practiced a simple and powerful method to plant forests of food: a small hollow in the soil, a touch of humus, a pinch of seeds.

That touch of fertility beneath each cluster of seeds sheltered and strengthened every sprout.

SeedballPlanet revives humanity’s simplest technology for rewilding the earth.

Our seedballs of clay, charcoal, and compost are the food forests of the future — ready to re‑plant even the most exhausted or invaded lands — almost no tools or irrigation.

We sell Seedballs — but anyone can make seedballs absolutely free.

We’re making food forestry global — with nothing more than a pinch of seeds and living compost.

“You want to reverse the damage? Just plant forests of foods.”

From one hand to a million drones, it’s the same act of renewal:

Plant the seed, feed the earth, let life do the rest.

SeedballPlanet.com — Everything for global regeneration.
