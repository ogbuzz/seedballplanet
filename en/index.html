Ancestral Roots: A Millennia-Old Wisdom

For thousands of years, long before industrial agriculture, the Indigenous peoples of the Amazon intentionally transformed their environment into abundant food forests. The Kayapo and other pre-Columbian peoples planted and maintained corridors of edible plants along their trails, scattered seeds into enriched soils, and shaped what we now call the “natural Amazon rainforest” — which is, in large part, a centuries-old human garden.

The most fascinating evidence of this ancestral knowledge is Terra Preta — literally “black earth” in Portuguese. These exceptionally fertile soils, discovered throughout the Amazon basin, are the direct result of the intentional incorporation of charcoal, calcined bones, and organic matter into the soil by these peoples. Unlike the naturally poor tropical soils surrounding them, Terra Preta has remained extraordinarily fertile after more than 2,000 years — and continues to regenerate on its own.

This is precisely the principle that SeedballPlanet reinvents with Poussbouls: the Canadian biochar we use in our formulation is the direct heir to this Amazonian wisdom, now validated by modern science.

The Poussbouls: Gardening Reinvented

Ever heard of guerrilla gardening? Those little seed balls tossed into abandoned lots? Poussbouls are so much more than that. They are scientifically formulated seedballs containing biochar, vermicompost, microbial inoculants, and mineralizing rock powders — designed to transform almost any space into a productive garden, effortlessly. This is what we call the Super-Poussboul — the world’s most advanced seedball.

For Urban Gardeners

Grow your own salads, kale, Asian leafy greens, and herbs from spring through late summer. On a balcony, in a pot, or on a lightly worked patch of ground — the Super-Poussboul adapts to your space. No experience needed, no tools required. One Super-Poussboul, a little soil, some water, and nature does the rest.

The Science Behind the Poussbouls

A Poussboul is an improved and optimized seedball built on modern technology. While remaining relatively simple to make, it is a complete blend of fertilizers and micro-organisms — designed to give every seed the best possible conditions to germinate and thrive. All ingredients used in Poussbouls are certified acceptable for organic agriculture.

Triple Bio-Dynamic Activation

The Super-Poussboul formulation combines three levels of biological activation: beneficial micro-organisms from inoculants made in Canada; high-quality vermicompost rich in bacteria and mycorrhizal fungi, safe for children and animals; and mineralizing rock powders that replenish the essential trace elements often absent from urban and degraded soils.

Canadian Biochar — the Modern Heir to Terra Preta

The biochar we use is a very high-quality Canadian charcoal that creates a permanent porous structure in the soil. It acts as a hotel for beneficial micro-organisms, improves water retention, and sequesters atmospheric carbon for hundreds of years — exactly as Terra Preta did more than 2,000 years ago.

Superior Growth from the Very Start

Our fertilizing ingredient mix produces remarkably strong growth in the very first days — outperforming competing formulations. This is consistent with the findings of Dr. Charles Ikenna Nwankwo, researcher at the University of Hohenheim in Germany, whose thesis demonstrates that the first two weeks of growth are critical to a plant’s long-term success.

A Growing Scientific Team

In 2026, we initiated discussions with a microbiologist specializing in living soil science, and we are seeking biologists with expertise in native plants to support the development of our Carbon Super-Poussboul. Our goal: achieve optimal seed emergence for every variety and every environment.

The SeedballPlanet Story — A Canadian Passion with Global Reach

Hello, my name is Sylvain Picker, founder of Jardin Odonata and creator of the Poussbouls. Thank you for supporting Canadian entrepreneurship.

A Path Rooted in the Land

After some twenty years in various roles in agriculture and forestry, and eleven years as a scientific instrumentation technician, I wanted to put that experience to work for a project I care deeply about: making nature productive, accessible, and fun for everyone.

The Birth of the Poussbouls

In 2017, with the collaboration of two scientists including Dr. Charles Ikenna Nwankwo from the University of Hohenheim in Germany, I formulated a truly high-performing seedball. We tested nearly 10,000 seedballs across a wide range of environments — fields, alleys, balconies, and indoor spaces — with spectacular results. These years of intensive research led to the creation of the Super-Poussboul, the world’s most advanced seedball.

Innovation with the Right Tools

My technical background also allowed me to design low-cost, efficient manufacturing tools capable of producing thousands of Poussbouls per day. This know-how is a unique competitive advantage — and the foundation of our vision: making Super-Poussboul production accessible to any ecological organization, school, or community that wants to produce its own seedballs, anywhere in the world.

Now Retired, Fully Committed

The Poussbouls are my retirement passion project — and that is precisely what allows me to give it the time, rigour, and patience it deserves. SeedballPlanet enters a virtually untapped Québec market with the ambition of becoming the province’s first structured commercial producer — and eventually, a global player.
