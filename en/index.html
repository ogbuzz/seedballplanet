Seedball Gardening Reinvented

Ever heard of guerrilla gardening? Those clay and compost balls full of seeds that are tossed into abandoned lots for growing flowers? Our new Seedballs are so much more than that. They are scientifically formulated and contain biochar, vermicompost, microbial inoculants, and mineralizing rock powders — designed to transform almost any space into a productive garden, effortlessly. This is the world’s most advanced Seedball.

For Urban Gardeners

Grow your own salads, kale, Asian leafy greens, and herbs from spring through late summer. On a balcony, in a pot, or on a lightly worked patch of ground — the Super-Poussboul adapts to your space. No experience needed, no tools required. One Super-Poussboul, a little soil, some water, and nature does the rest.

For Pollinator Flowers

Flowers for butterflies, bees, and other pollinators—as well as for birds—can be planted almost any time during the growing season. This field was planted in early July 2017 during a very dry year, without any irrigation. In the same trial, we also grew a variety of lettuces, herbs, and Asian vegetables.

For Those Who Love to Grow

In Quebec, you can plant salads, Asian leafy greens, kales, and several herbs from April to August. With Seedballs, planting is quick and arranged in an easy-to-weed pattern (in small clusters). To grow greens all season, use succession planting by planting new seeds every 2–3 weeks.

For Ecological Restoration

Our high-biochar Carbon Super-Poussboul scheduled for next fall, sequester a significant amount of CO₂ in the soil while helping native plants resist predators more effectively. Ideal for restoring hiking trails, riverbanks, and for planting wild leek, ginseng, and native species in maple groves.

For the World food emergency

SeedballPlanet is developing a scalable and natural solution: SuperFood Seedballs — an accessible model for regenerative and nutritious food production, starting from everyone’s soil.

After several years of research and prototyping, we have already created tools capable of producing thousands of SuperFood Seedballs by hand.

Our Seedballs stand out thanks to their high biochar content, which allows them to permanently sequester CO₂ while enhancing soil structure and fertility.

Our next goal is clear: to scale up both industrially and ecologically, with modular machines capable of producing millions of Seedballs each season, to restore food security, the climate, and ecosystems worldwide.
