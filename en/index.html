Join us in restoring Earth's fertility and food productivity by starting food-forests using ancient, nearly tool‑free methods.

For thousands of years, long before agriculture, humans used a simple technique to regenerate ecosystems and grow food: placing a pinch of seeds mixed with humus in small soil hollows and letting nature do most of the work. This ancient approach created abundant "food forests", such as those still found in the Amazon and Asia.

SeedballPlanet revives this ancient method with modern practicality – making clay, charcoal, and compost seedballs containing fast growing leafy greens, herbs, fruits, and mushroom seeds. These self‑contained "mini ecosystems" can re‑grow degraded land with almost no tools, irrigation, or maintenance. Start eating fresh greens immediately while building tomorrow's self-sustaining ecosystem.

Our mission: global food reforestation by turning Earth into self-sustaining food forests.

Start today: buy ready-made seedballs – or learn to craft your own for free.
